CHEST WORKOUT

  • Bench Press: This is a classic compound movement that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. You can do it using a barbell or dumbbells
  • Push-Ups: A bodyweight exercise that effectively works the chest muscles. You can modify the difficulty by adjusting hand placement or elevating your feet
  • Dumbbell Flyes: Lie on a bench and perform flyes by bringing the dumbbells together in a controlled manner, targeting the pectoral muscles
  • Incline Bench Press: Similar to the bench press, but performed on an inclined bench. This targets the upper chest
  • Chest Dips: Using parallel bars, dip down to work your chest and triceps. You can lean forward slightly to emphasize the chest more
  • Cable Crossovers: Performed on a cable machine, this exercise involves crossing the cables in front of your body to target the chest muscles.
  • Pec Deck Machine: This machine simulates a flye motion, allowing you to isolate and work the chest muscles
  • Push-Up Variations: Aside from regular push-ups, you can try wide-grip push-ups, diamond push-ups, and decline push-ups to target different areas of the chest
  • Single-Arm Dumbbell Press: Pressing a dumbbell with one arm at a time adds an element of instability and requires more core engagement
