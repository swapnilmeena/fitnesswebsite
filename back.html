BACK WORKOUT

  • Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups: These compound exercises target the upper back, particularly the lats and biceps.
  • Deadlifts: A powerful compound movement that engages the entire back, along with the lower body and core.
  • Bent-Over Rows: These work the upper and middle back, promoting muscle growth and strength.
  • Lat Pulldowns: A variation of pull-ups, this exercise targets the lats and upper back muscles.
  • Seated Cable Rows: Effective for targeting the middle back and improving posture.
  • Face Pulls: These focus on the rear deltoids and upper back, aiding in shoulder health.
  • Hyperextensions/Back Extensions: Strengthen the erector spinae muscles and promote lower back health.
  • T-Bar Rows: Engage the upper back, lats, and traps, contributing to a well-rounded back workout.
  • Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows: Effective for isolating and strengthening the lats and upper back.
