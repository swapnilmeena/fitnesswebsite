TRICEPS WORKOUT

  • Push-Ups: Regular push-ups target the triceps along with the chest and shoulders. To emphasize the triceps, keep your hands closer together.
  • Triceps Dips: You can do these using parallel bars or a stable elevated surface. Lower your body by bending your elbows and then push back up using your triceps.
  • Close-Grip Bench Press: Lie down on a bench and hold the barbell with a narrow grip. Lower the barbell to your chest and then push it back up using your triceps.
  • Skull Crushers: Lie on a bench, hold a barbell or dumbbells above your chest, and lower the weight towards your forehead. Extend your arms back up to work the triceps.
  • Triceps Kickbacks: Hold a dumbbell in one hand and lean forward slightly. Extend your arm behind you, focusing on contracting the triceps. Alternate between arms.
  • Cable Pushdowns: Use a cable machine with a rope attachment or a bar. Keep your upper arms stationary and push the attachment downward using your triceps.
  • Diamond Push-Ups: Form a diamond shape with your hands by touching your thumbs and index fingers. Perform push-ups, keeping your elbows close to your body to target the triceps.
  • Triceps Rope Pulldown: Attach a rope to a cable machine and grasp the ends of the rope. Pull the rope downward while keeping your elbows close to your body.
