FOREARMS WORKOUT

  • Wrist Curls with Dumbbell:
  • Sit on a bench or chair with your forearm resting on your thigh or a bench.
  • Hold a dumbbell in your hand with an underhand grip (palms facing up)
    • .
  • Allow your wrist to extend down, and then slowly curl it up by flexing your wrist.
  • Lower the dumbbell back down with control.
  • Repetitions: 12-15 curls per forearm.
  • Reverse Wrist Curls with Dumbbell:
  • Similar to the previous exercise, but this time use an overhand grip (palms facing down) on the dumbbell.
  • Allow your wrist to flex down, and then slowly curl it up by extending your wrist.
  • Lower the dumbbell back down with control.
  • Repetitions: 12-15 curls per forearm.
  • Hammer Curls:
  • Hold a dumbbell in each hand with a neutral grip (palms facing your body).
  • Keeping your upper arms stationary, curl the dumbbells while contracting your forearms.
  • Squeeze your forearms at the top of the movement, then lower the dumbbells back down.
  • Repetitions: 10-12 curls.
  • Farmers Walk:
  • Hold a heavy dumbbell in each hand by your sides.
  • Walk for a set distance (e.g., 50 feet) or time while maintaining a strong grip on the dumbbells.
  • Focus on keeping your posture upright and engaging your core as you walk
    • .
  • Duration: 2-3 rounds of 50 feet or 30-45 seconds each.
  • Plate Pinches:
  • Hold two weight plates (or similar flat objects with handles) using your fingertips and thumb.
  • Pinch the plates together and hold them at your sides.
  • Maintain the pinch grip for a set time.
  • Duration: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds.
