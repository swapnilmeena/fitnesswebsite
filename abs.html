ABS WORKOUT

  • Warm-up:
  • Start with 5-10 minutes of light cardio, such as jogging in place or jumping jacks, to get your blood flowing and your muscles warmed up.
  • Abs Workout:
  • This workout consists of five exercises that target different areas of your abdominal muscles. Perform each exercise for the recommended number of repetitions or time, and then move on to the next exercise. Complete the entire circuit, and then rest for 1-2 minutes before repeating the circuit 2-3 times.
  • Crunches:
  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
  • Place your hands behind your head or crossed over your chest.
  • Engage your core and lift your upper back off the ground, curling your shoulders towards your hips.
  • Lower yourself back down with control, but don't let your head touch the ground.
  • Repetitions: 15-20 crunches.
  • Leg Raises:
  • Lie on your back with your legs extended and your arms by your sides.
  • Keeping your legs straight, lift them up towards the ceiling, then lower them back down without letting them touch the floor.
  • Keep your lower back pressed into the ground throughout the movement.
  • Repetitions: 12-15 leg raises.
  • Plank:
  • Start in a push-up position, but with your weight on your forearms instead of your hands.
  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels, engaging your core and glutes.
  • Hold this position for as long as you can maintain proper form.
  • Time: Aim for 30-60 seconds.
  • Russian Twists:
  • Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
  • Lean back slightly, keeping your back straight and your core engaged.
  • Hold a weight or a household object with both hands, and twist your torso to one side, then the other.
  • Repetitions: 20 twists (10 each side).
  • Bicycle Crunches:
  • Lie on your back with your hands behind your head and your knees lifted towards your chest.
  • Alternate bringing one elbow towards the opposite knee while extending the other leg.
  • Keep your core engaged and your movements controlled.
  • Repetitions: 20 bicycle crunches (10 each side).
